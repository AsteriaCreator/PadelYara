# PadelYara — Competitor Tracker

Last updated: 2026-06-06

---

## Framing

PadelYara has no direct Austrian competitors. It is the only padel court aggregator in the DACH market. The competitors below are framed by threat level and learning value, not by direct competition.

---

## Primary Reference: Playskan (UK)

**URL:** playskan.com
**Positioning:** "Skyscanner for padel courts" — UK market
**Status:** Active, growing, has press coverage

**Why it matters:**
Playskan is the proof of concept. They validated the aggregator model in the UK. PadelYara is the Austrian version. What they do well = what we should do. Where they have gaps = our opportunity.

**What to monitor:**
- Are they expanding to DACH?
- What features are they shipping?
- How do they handle the venue partnership angle?
- What does their content strategy look like?

**Threat level:** Low currently (UK only). Medium if they announce DACH expansion.
**Action if they expand:** Move faster on SEO and brand recognition. We have home-field advantage.

**Last checked:** 2026-06-06
**Notes:** UK only. No German UX. No Austrian venue coverage. Gap is wide.

**Last checked:** 2026-06-06

## Adjacent Threat: Eversports

**URL:** eversports.com
**What they do:** Austrian/DACH club management + booking platform
**Why they matter:** They own the venue relationship for many Austrian padel clubs. If they build cross-venue search, they compete directly.

**Current state:** No cross-platform search. Siloed by design — each club on their own profile.
**Threat level:** Medium — they have the venue relationships, they just haven't built aggregation
**Watch for:** Any announcement of cross-venue search or aggregation features
**Last checked:** 2026-06-06

---

## Adjacent Threat: eTennis

**URL:** etennis.at

**Why they matter:** Same as Eversports — owns venue relationships, could build cross-venue search

**Current state:** Siloed by design
**Threat level:** Medium
**Watch for:** Any UX changes suggesting aggregation intent
**Last checked:** 2026-06-06

## Adjacent Threat: Playtomic

**URL:** playtomic.io
**What they do:** Global padel booking platform + social features
**Why they matter:** They are the 800-pound gorilla globally. NOT used in Austria currently.

**Current state:** Not present in Austrian market
**Threat level:** High IF they enter Austria — they have scale, brand, and technology
**Watch for:** Any Austrian venue signing up to Playtomic. Even one venue is a signal.
**Action if they enter:** Double down on local identity. "Built in Austria, for Austria" is a moat Playtomic cannot replicate.
**Last checked:** 2026-06-06

## What Would Trigger an Alarm

1. Playskan announces German-language version or DACH expansion
2. Any Austrian venue signs up to Playtomic
3. Eversports or eTennis adds cross-venue search
4. A new Austrian startup launches a padel aggregator

If any of these happen: immediate strategy review.
