# PadelYara — Marketing Strategy

Last updated: 2026-06-06

---

## The One Thing

Yara räumt das österreichische Padel-Chaos auf.

Every marketing decision is tested against this. If something does not support this association, it does not ship.

---

## Stage

**Pre-launch / organic seeding.**
MVP is live. Brand exists but is not yet known. No budget. The goal right now is not scale — it is planting seeds in the right soil.

---

## Strategic Priorities (ordered)

1. **Own "Padel Wien" search intent** — SEO foundation before competitors notice the gap
2. **Seed the real community** — WhatsApp groups, Facebook groups, padel clubs where actual players are
3. **Build the Yara persona publicly** — Instagram as the long-term brand channel
4. **Earn club relationships** — direct outreach, they want players to find them
5. **Build content infrastructure** — backlog, posting rhythm, reusable formats

---

## Positioning

**Core claim:**
One search. Every court in Austria. Regardless of booking platform.

**Secondary claim:**
Built by a player, for players. No corporate bullshit.

**What we are not:**
- A booking platform (we never touch the booking — we just find the court)
- A generic sports app
- An influencer brand

**Market reference frame:**
PadelYara is what Playskan (UK) is to British padel — but built for Austria, in German, by someone who plays here.

---

## Brand Hierarchy

1. Character: Yara (the cat, the voice, the mascot)
2. Mission: Remove friction from Austrian padel
3. Product: Court aggregator (now), stats + merch (later)

The character leads. The product follows.

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Austrian padel players, 30–54, Vienna region, tech-comfortable, play 1–3x/week
**Behavioral signal:** Makes same-day or next-day court decisions. Frustrated by multiple booking tabs.
**Where they are:** Padel WhatsApp groups, Austrian padel Facebook groups, Instagram, clubs in Vienna / NÖ / Wr. Neustadt region

**Gender:** ~60% male, ~40% female. Do not design only for men.

| Channel | Priority | Role |
|---|---|---|

## Success Metrics (not vanity)

**Short term (next 90 days):**
- 50 organic users / week
- Listed in at least 3 Austrian padel Facebook groups without being removed
- First direct club relationship established
- At least 5 pieces of content published on Instagram

**Medium term (6 months):**
- 200 organic users / week
- Top 3 Google result for "Padel Wien Court buchen" or equivalent
- Instagram: 500 followers (real, no growth hacking)
- At least 2 clubs actively promoting PadelYara to their players

**Long term (12 months):**
- Brand recognition: name recall in Austrian padel community
- Revenue from first income stream (featured listing or merch)
- 1000+ weekly active users

---

## Budget

Zero.

Every strategy must work without paid media.

When first income materializes, first spend goes to:
1. Occasional boosted Instagram post (brand awareness, not lead gen)
2. Content creation tools if needed

---

## What We Will Not Do

- Paid influencer partnerships (unless genuinely aligned, organic relationship)
- Generic padel content — tips, "did you know" posts, match highlights
- Giveaways or follower-bait mechanics
- Cross-posting without adapting for platform
- English content for the Austrian audience
- Anything that makes Yara sound warm, enthusiastic, or corporate
