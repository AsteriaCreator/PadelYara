# Yara — Visual Character Brief

*For illustrators, designers, and AI image generation. Companion to `.agents/yara-voice.md` (the written voice). The character on the page and the character in the copy must be the same cat.*

**The character is based on the founder's real cat, Yara** — see reference photos in `brand/photos/` (`yaraoncourt.png` is the canonical hero pose, `therealPadelyara.jpg` a second angle). Not the logo. The logo is a separate stylized mark; this avatar is *the actual cat, stylized*.

---

## One line

A sleek black cat with green-gold eyes and standards. She looked at your padel game, formed an opinion, and is not going to pretend otherwise.

---

## Who she is (read this first)

Yara is the brand's mascot and narrator — **competent, unbothered, mean** the way a cat is mean: not cruel, just honest. She fixed a problem nobody else would (too many booking tabs) and she's slightly annoyed she had to. She is *her own character*, not a portrait of the founder.

Everything visual must read as **confidence and contempt at rest** — never cute, never bubbly, never trying to be liked. Good news: the real Yara already has this face. The court photo *is* the brief.

---

## Species & form — the real Yara

- **A solid-black domestic cat.** Short-haired, sleek, lean, elegant. Glossy near-black coat.
- **Green-gold eyes** — bright, striking against the black. This is her single most identifying feature. (See color section — likely the one accent color.)
- **Ears:** large, tall, pointed, alert.
- **Muzzle:** fine, with white whiskers catching light against the black fur.
- **Build:** lean and poised. She sits bolt upright and still, like she's already decided you're wrong.
- Padel cues are *native to her*, not costume: the red-and-black padel racket and a neon tennis ball appear naturally in her reference photos. Use them as dry punctuation — a paw resting on the racket, a ball owned under one paw.

---

## Expression & attitude — the whole game

This is where she lives or dies. Get the face right and everything follows. The real Yara already nails it in `yaraoncourt.png` — study it.

- **Eyes:** open but level and direct. The unimpressed, "I saw your smash" stare. Calm, not wide or sparkly.
- **Brow/muzzle:** the faint flatness of mild disapproval. Composed, not tense.
- **Mouth:** closed, neutral. **No open-mouth smiles. No tongue. No :3.**
- **Posture:** upright, still, weight settled, tail curled in. Stillness is the power move — she doesn't gesture.
- **Default emotion:** flat assessment. If you have to label it: *"and?"*

---

## Color & rendering

**Recommended direction (flag this with the founder before final art):**

- **Near-monochrome with green-gold eyes as the single accent.** The coat, background, and props render in the brand greys — near-black `#111111`, white `#ffffff`, silver-grey `#cccccc`, mid-grey `#555555` — and **only the eyes carry color**. One deliberate pop in an otherwise black-and-white brand. True to the real cat *and* a strong graphic move.
- *Optional second accent:* the neon tennis ball, used sparingly, when a shot needs one warm point of energy.
- *Alternative if she prefers:* strict pure-monochrome (eyes in grey too) — cleaner, but loses what makes the real Yara recognizable. Not recommended.

**Rendering style:**
- **Stylized, not photoreal.** Flat / graphic / minimal illustration — clean shapes, limited shading, high contrast. A black cat is *already* a near-silhouette, which suits this style perfectly.
- **Works at any size** — readable as a tiny profile photo and as a large header. The silhouette (sleek black body, tall ears, upright sit) should be recognizable in solid black, with the eyes as the focal glow.
- Backgrounds: solid `#111111` or `#ffffff`, or transparent. No busy scenes.

---

## Pose / shot library (the recurring set)

Build a small, reusable set so she's consistent across a feed:

1. **The Look** — front-facing, direct level stare. The profile-picture pose. (≈ `yaraoncourt.png`.)
2. **Seated, unbothered** — sitting bolt upright, tail curled, surveying.
3. **Paw on ball** — one paw resting on a tennis ball, owning it.
4. **Over-the-shoulder glance** — turning back at you, mid-judgment.
5. **Racket, claimed** — sitting on / beside the red-black racket like it's hers and beneath her.

Keep the *face, eye color, and proportions identical* across all of them — that's what makes it read as one cat.

---

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Sleek solid-black cat, true to the photos | Fluffy, brown, tabby, or fox-like |
| Bright green-gold eyes as the accent | Wide sparkly anime eyes; eyes the wrong color |
| Flat, graphic, high-contrast illustration | Photoreal fur; heavy painterly render |
| Direct, level, unimpressed gaze | "Fun," excited, or pleading energy |
| Still, settled, upright posture | Action poses, mid-jump |
| Neutral closed mouth | Open smiles, tongues, hearts |
| Recognizable silhouette in solid black | Fussy detail that dies when shrunk |
| Racket/ball as dry punctuation | Costumes, headbands, sweatbands |

## Consistency anchors (for AI generation)

The hard part with AI is "same cat, new pose." Lock these and repeat them in every prompt:

- **Species/style:** stylized flat illustration of a sleek solid-black cat, minimal/high-contrast
- **Signature:** bright green-gold eyes (the only accent color), tall pointed ears, fine white whiskers
- **Palette:** black / white / grey everywhere except the green-gold eyes
- **Attitude keyword:** *unimpressed, contemptuous, composed, direct stare*
- **Use the real photo.** Feed `brand/photos/yaraoncourt.png` as an image reference / character reference so the model matches the actual cat, then generate a strong front-facing "hero" version. Drive every other pose from that hero image rather than re-rolling from text.

### Starter prompt

> Flat, high-contrast vector illustration of a sleek solid-black domestic cat, stylized and minimal. Tall pointed ears, fine white whiskers, bright green-gold eyes as the only spot of color. Calm, level, unimpressed, composed expression — looking directly at the viewer. Sitting upright and still, poised. Monochrome black/white/grey palette except the green-gold eyes. Solid background. No gradients elsewhere, no extra colors, not cute, not chibi, not photoreal.

Adjust the **pose line** per shot; keep everything else fixed.

## Sanity check before approving any art

1. Does the face look like it just judged your backhand? ✅
2. Sleek solid-black cat with green-gold eyes — recognizably *her*? ✅
3. Black/white/grey everywhere except the eyes? ✅
4. Recognizable as a tiny profile pic? ✅
5. Zero cuteness, zero smiling? ✅

If any answer is no, it's not Yara yet.
