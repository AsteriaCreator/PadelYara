Last updated: 2026-06-06

## Framework

Each experiment:
- **Hypothesis** — what we expect to happen
- **Action** — what to do
- **Measure** — how to know if it worked
- **Status** — Not started / Running / Done / Abandoned
- **Result** — fill in after

One experiment active at a time unless clearly independent.

## Experiment 01 — WhatsApp Group Seeding

**Hypothesis:** One genuine message in the right padel WhatsApp group generates the first real users.

**Action:**
The founder shares PadelYara once in her personal padel WhatsApp group(s). Natural message, player tone:
> "Ich hab da was gebaut — falls ihr Court-Suche-Chaos kennt: padelyara.com findet Courts von eTennis und Eversports auf einmal. Noch beta aber es funktioniert."

**Measure:**
- Spike in users on day of message
- Any replies / questions
- Whether the link gets shared further

## Experiment 02 — Austrian Padel Facebook Groups

**Hypothesis:** A non-promotional, genuinely helpful introduction post in Austrian padel Facebook groups gets traction without backlash.

**Action:**
1. Map all relevant Austrian padel Facebook groups (research task below)
2. Join each as the founder
3. Post once — as a player sharing something useful, not as a marketer
4. Message template:
   > "Hallo! Ich bin Cornelia, spiele Padel in der Gegend Wien/NÖ und hab ein kleines Tool gebaut weil mich das ewige Tab-Chaos genervt hat: padelyara.com — zeigt freie Courts von eTennis und Eversports in einer Suche. Noch im Aufbau, aber vielleicht hilfreich für die Gruppe!"

## Experiment 03 — Direct Club Outreach

**Hypothesis:** Padel club admins or venue managers want players to find their courts. They will share PadelYara if we simply tell them it exists.

**Action:**
1. List all venues currently in PadelYara (from Padel_Venues.csv)
2. Find contact info for each (website, Instagram, email)
3. Send a short, direct message:
   > "Hallo! Ich betreibe PadelYara (padelyara.com) — eine Plattform, die eure Courts automatisch in der Suche zeigt, wenn sie auf eTennis oder Eversports verfügbar sind. Ihr müsst nichts tun, es läuft bereits. Falls ihr es euren Spieler:innen empfehlen wollt — gerne. Kostenlos, immer."

**Measure:**
- Any clubs share or mention PadelYara to their players
- Club Instagram accounts tag or mention PadelYara
- Traffic from club website referrals

**Status:** Not started
**Result:** —
